Helios Packaging manufactures and produces a wide array of packaging materials, customized products, and solutions to cater to the needs of numerous sector-specific industries.

Air Bubble Film

This material provides excellent cushioning, void-fill properties, and guaranteed protection against shock, scratches, and vibration during transit.

Angle Boards

These are 100% recyclable products that provide additional protection and strength to carton edges and corners during transit along with greater load stability.

Composite Packaging

Composite Packaging is basically packaging solutions that comprise a mix and match of different packaging materials to ensure greater product protection during transportation.

Corrugated Cartons

These provide protection during transportation along with additional inner bracing and cushioning to protect fragile components.

EPE Foam & Fitments

These are made with eco-friendly flexible material which provides excellent thermal insulation, elasticity, shock absorption, and are chemical and weather resistant.

Foldable Large Container – FLC

These are injection molded heavy-duty  HDPE crates that can be reused multiple times and are convenient for safely transporting heavier loads.

HDPE Crates & Bins

These are injection molded HDPE containers that can be used for storage, transportation, distribution, and handling of numerous goods and products.

Pallet Sleeve Boxes

These are reusable, durable, lightweight foldable boxes that are designed specially to carry loads up to 600 Kgs with a life cycle of approximately ten years.

Polypropylene Sheets & Boxes

This material is extremely chemical and weather resistant, apart from this they are also extremely durable, strong, reusable, and has a longer product life cycle.

Polyurethane Foam & Fitments

These are made using low-density foam sheets in rigid or flexible forms and provide excellent cushioning and anti-static protection for goods during transit.

Stretch Film Rolls

These are highly malleable, elastic film rolls that are used to unitize pallet loads to keep the products tightly bound and provide protection from moisture and dust.

Wooden Pallets

These provide greater stability during the loading and transportation of heavier goods and enable easier handling and transit.

XLPE Foam & Fitments

These provide excellent cushioning and anti-static protection to fragile products during transit and are available in different densities and thicknesses.
